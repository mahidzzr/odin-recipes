Milk Egg Caramel Pudding

Description

Milk Egg Caramel Pudding, also known as cr√®me caramel or flan, is a classic dessert characterized by its smooth, creamy texture and rich caramel flavor. The dessert features a layer of golden, sweet caramel sauce atop a soft and silky custard base made from milk, eggs, and sugar. The custard is baked in a water bath to ensure even cooking and a delicate, jiggly consistency. When served, the pudding is inverted onto a plate, allowing the caramel to flow over the custard, creating a delightful contrast between the creamy custard and the sweet, slightly bitter caramel.

Ingredients

  • For the Caramel:
    • 1 cup (200g) sugar
    • 1/4 cup (60ml) water
  • For the Custard:
    • 2 cups (480ml) whole milk
    • 4 large eggs
    • 1/2 cup(100g) suger
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
